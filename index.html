Kote Shri Veera Anjaneya Temple

About Temple

Kote Shree Veeranjaneya Temple is located at Arikady village belongs to Manjeshwar block refer to Kumbla (sometimes Kumbala or Kumble) panchayat comes under Kasaragod district in the Indian state of Kerala.


It is located roughly 6 kms from the taluka headquarters Manjeshwar, about 12 kms towards north from district headquarters Kasaragod, approximately 11 kms from Uppala and around 40 kms from Mangalore.


This is the most famous temple of Lord Hanuman with Shree Mallikarjuna situated at Ikkeri Fort, Hanuman Nagar on National Highway 17.


The brightly painted life size sculpture of Lord Veera Anjaneya holding mace in left hand and giving blessings as Abhaya Hasta is positioned at just front of the entrance.


Saffron coloured temple with big lamps tower looks mind blowing during late evening.
